2. Stage tracked files and commit with a clear message.
3. Push the commit to `origin/main`.

## 10 New Feature & Game Ideas for SylvieApp

1. **Magic Story Builder**
   - Let kids choose characters, settings, and gentle actions to create their own short story.
   - Add a “read aloud” mode with calming narration and highlight text.

2. **Calm Craft Studio**
   - A creative art space for making digital cards, stickers, and glittery frames.
   - Add drag-and-drop stickers, color gradients, and save/share to parent settings.

3. **Friendship Garden Expansion**
   - Add new garden zones like a fairy pond or cozy treehouse.
   - Unlock decorations and soft mini-challenges by collecting stars or kindness points.

4. **Relaxing Rhythm Game**
   - Tap or press gentle beats in time with soothing music.
   - Include a “slow mode” for little hands and a reward system for consistency.

5. **Puzzle Adventure Map**
   - Connect all puzzle games via a colorful map with new areas to unlock.
   - Each completed game reveals a new story location or friendly character.

6. **Dress-Up Party Mode**
   - Expand princess dress-up with background scenes, party themes, and photo booth snapshots.
   - Add mix-and-match accessories, seasonal outfits, and a “show and tell” end card.

7. **Animal Care Farm Helper**
   - Add feeding, grooming, and bedtime routines for more farm animals.
   - Include a “pet diary” where kids can collect stickers for caring actions.

8. **Quiet Focus Challenges**
   - Short, guided activities for breathing, stretching, or calming observation.
   - Include a “timer + gentle reminder” feature with animated prompts and soft encouragement.

9. **Treasure Hunt Explorer**
   - A simple scavenger hunt across game screens with hidden stars or items.
   - Add hints and positive feedback for every discovery to keep it encouraging.

10. **Parent Dashboard Upgrades**
    - Add progress summaries, star history, and favorite activity tracking.
    - Let parents create custom encouragement themes, bedtime messages, or reward goals.

These ideas keep the app playful and gentle while expanding creativity, movement, calm focus, and ongoing discovery.
