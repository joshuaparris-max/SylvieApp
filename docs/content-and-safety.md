# SylvieApp Content and Safety

## Audience

SylvieApp is designed for calm preschool play with parent awareness. It emphasizes imaginative play, creativity, kindness, movement breaks, and gentle persistence.

## Safety Commitments

- No child-facing external links.
- No ads.
- No accounts or login.
- No purchases.
- No social sharing.
- Sounds are off by default.
- Rewards are gentle and non-pressuring.
- All characters, scenes, and illustrations are original.
- Browser-local progress only.

## Originality Rules

The app is inspired by broad play themes rather than specific franchises. Do not add:

- official character names
- franchise names
- logos
- copyrighted art
- copyrighted music
- official voices or impersonations

Use descriptive original themes instead, such as "ocean princess", "ice castle sisters", or "book-loving princess".

## Parent Settings

The default parent passcode is:

```text
2468
```

Stored data includes stars, farm progress, outfit state, saved drawings, block creations, puzzle progress, story progress, sorting progress, and parent settings.

## Content Tone

Use:

- calm encouragement
- short instructions
- kind retry language
- soft movement prompts
- non-scary stories
- no pressure to complete
