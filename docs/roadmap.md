# SylvieApp Roadmap

This roadmap captures the original idea list in a cleaner product format. Additions should stay gentle, original, and child-safe.

## Priority 1: Parent Confidence and Polish

- Keep README and docs synchronized after major changes.
- Run `npm run build` after each major update.
- Add a small manual QA checklist for common routes.
- Add parent-facing progress summaries, favorite activity tracking, and star history.

## Priority 2: Deeper Creative Play

- Magic Story Builder: choose characters, settings, and gentle actions to create a short story.
- Calm Craft Studio: make cards, stickers, frames, and saved craft pictures.
- Dress-Up Party Mode: backgrounds, party themes, photo booth snapshots, and end cards.
- Puzzle Adventure Map: connect puzzle games through unlockable story locations.

## Priority 3: Movement, Calm, and Care

- Friendship Garden Expansion: new garden zones such as a pond or treehouse.
- Animal Care Farm Helper: feeding, grooming, bedtime routines, and a pet diary.
- Quiet Focus Challenges: breathing, stretching, calm observation, and timers.
- Relaxing Rhythm Game: slow-mode beat tapping with soft feedback.

## Priority 4: Discovery

- Treasure Hunt Explorer with hidden stars or items across game screens.
- More original favorite-picture themes and saved creations.
- Parent-created encouragement themes, bedtime messages, or reward goals.

## Already Implemented From Earlier Ideas

- Fairy friend theme.
- Cozy harvest farm.
- Original piglet, puppy, princess, mermaid, mouse, and sorting truck themes through favorite pictures.
- Colouring table.
- Garden swing and trampoline movement break.
- Dress-up items and magic wands.
- Puzzle pieces.
- Block tower.
